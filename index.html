Welcome to Catspell Cat Resort

Catspell is an excellent boarding facility specializing in Cat Care. Our mission is to reassure clients that their Cats will be kept in a healthy, stimulating, safe and sound environment by experienced carers who will look after their every need and whim.

Our high expectations at Catspell instill client’s trust that the welfare of their Cats is safeguarded in our unique establishment. You are welcome to call us to arrange an inspection, or to discuss the details of your Cat’s stay with us.
